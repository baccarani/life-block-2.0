Overview of Life Block

  • Life Block is a protocol that provides a decentralized alternatetive to insurance.

  • Life insurance policies are automated via blockchain, smart contract, and Soulbound Token (SBT) technology.

  • Death certificates are published on the blockchain and will automatically trigger the smart contract (aka. the life insurance policy on the blockchain) to initiate the death benefit pay-out process.
